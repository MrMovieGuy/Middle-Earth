Hugo Weaving has no plans to return as Elrond for upcoming Lord of the Rings film.

Hugo Weaving has recently stated that he had been fed up of the Lord of the Rings francise as he told GamesRadar+,
"It was great being in New Zealand on and off for a 10-year period."
"I did go back and work with the same team on a project called Mortal Engines, which was going to be their next big franchise, but it died on its arse. So, I've had enough of Middle-earth. I don't imagine anyone would ask me to do it again."

10th October 2024
